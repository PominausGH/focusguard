Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

Overview

FocusShield is designed with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device.

Data We Collect

FocusShield stores all data locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on our servers.

  • Tasks you create
  • Focus session history
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services. No cookies are used for tracking purposes.

Third-Party Services

FocusShield does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or using the sign-out feature in the app.

Questions? Reach out via GitHub.
