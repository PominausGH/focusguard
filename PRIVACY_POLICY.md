# Privacy Policy for FocusGuard

**Last Updated:** January 26, 2026

## Introduction

FocusGuard ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Email address and display name when you create an account
- **Task Data:** Tasks you create, including titles, completion status, and dates
- **Settings:** Your preferences such as default salary for meeting calculations and theme preferences

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Analytics:** Anonymous usage statistics including:
  - Number of tasks completed
  - Focus session durations
  - Meeting cost calculations
- **Device Information:** Device type and operating system (for app compatibility)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the FocusGuard service
- Save your tasks and settings across sessions
- Track your productivity statistics (locally on your device)
- Improve our application based on anonymous usage patterns

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

All your personal data (tasks, settings, analytics) is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms. We do not transmit this data to external servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync features.

### Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you choose to enable cloud sync:

- Your data is stored securely using Firebase/Supabase infrastructure
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- You can delete your cloud data at any time

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share anonymous, aggregated data for:

- Improving our services
- Research and analytics

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** View all data we have about you
- **Delete:** Remove your account and all associated data
- **Export:** Download your data in a portable format (CSV export available)
- **Opt-out:** Disable analytics tracking in settings

## Data Retention

- **Local Data:** Stored until you delete the app or clear app data
- **Cloud Data:** Retained until you delete your account
- **Analytics:** Anonymized data may be retained for up to 2 years

## Children's Privacy

FocusGuard is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

- Secure local storage (AsyncStorage/SecureStore)
- HTTPS encryption for all network communications
- Input validation and sanitization
- No storage of sensitive credentials in plain text

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Posting the new policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
